# Executive Summary

Campus Connect is a student-led digital campus ecosystem designed to unify campus operations, engagement, communication, resources, events, opportunities and analytics into a single modern platform.

The platform should feel like a premium SaaS product rather than a traditional college portal.

Design inspiration:

* Linear
* Stripe
* Vercel
* Notion
* Apple
