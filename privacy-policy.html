Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Campus Connect collects, uses and protects information.

1. Introduction

Campus Connect ("Platform", "Service", "We", "Our", or "Us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, disclosed and safeguarded when you use Campus Connect and related services. By accessing or using the platform, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the platform, we may collect the following information:

  • Name and profile information
  • Email address and contact details
  • Educational institution information
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Support and communication records
  • Usage analytics and interaction data
  • Device and browser information
  • Technical logs and diagnostic information

3. Information You Provide

Information may be provided directly by users when:

  • Creating an account
  • Submitting forms
  • Contacting support
  • Providing feedback
  • Participating in surveys
  • Using communication features

4. How We Use Information

Information collected through Campus Connect may be used for the following purposes:

  • Providing platform functionality
  • Managing user accounts
  • Improving user experience
  • Delivering notifications and updates
  • Responding to support requests
  • Monitoring platform performance
  • Enhancing security and reliability
  • Developing new features and services
  • Complying with legal obligations

5. Usage Analytics

We may collect anonymized usage statistics to understand platform performance, feature adoption and user engagement. Analytics information is generally used to improve functionality and user experience.

6. Cookies & Similar Technologies

Campus Connect may use cookies, local storage and similar technologies to improve platform performance and user experience.

These technologies may be used for:

  • User authentication
  • Session management
  • Remembering preferences
  • Performance optimization
  • Analytics and reporting
  • Security monitoring

Users may configure browser settings to control or disable cookies, although some features may not function properly.

7. Third-Party Services

Campus Connect may integrate with trusted third-party services that support platform functionality.

Examples may include:

  • Email delivery services
  • Analytics providers
  • Cloud hosting platforms
  • Authentication services
  • Customer support systems
  • Form processing services

These providers may process information solely for the purpose of delivering their respective services.

8. Information Sharing

Campus Connect does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary:

  • To provide requested services
  • With authorized institutional administrators
  • With trusted service providers
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect platform security and integrity
  • To prevent fraud or misuse

9. User Rights

Depending on applicable laws and regulations, users may have certain rights regarding their information.

These rights may include:

  • Accessing personal information
  • Requesting corrections to inaccurate information
  • Requesting deletion of information where permitted
  • Restricting certain processing activities
  • Objecting to specific uses of information
  • Requesting a copy of personal information

Requests related to privacy rights may be submitted through our official contact channels.

10. Data Security

Campus Connect takes reasonable administrative, technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration or disclosure.

Security practices may include:

  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Secure infrastructure and hosting environments
  • Regular platform monitoring
  • Security reviews and updates
  • Restricted access to sensitive information
  • Incident response procedures

While we strive to protect information, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

11. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce platform policies.

Retention periods may vary depending on:

  • Account activity
  • Institutional requirements
  • Legal obligations
  • Operational and security needs

12. Children's Privacy

Campus Connect is intended primarily for educational communities and institutional users.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children where prohibited by applicable law. If we become aware that information has been collected inappropriately, reasonable steps will be taken to remove such information.

13. International Data Processing

Where applicable, information may be processed through infrastructure, service providers or systems located in different regions.

Appropriate safeguards will be implemented to protect information during such processing.

14. Policy Updates

Campus Connect may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect platform changes, legal requirements or operational improvements.

When significant changes are made, the updated version will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

Continued use of the platform after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

15. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, data protection practices or privacy-related requests, please contact us through the following channel:

  • Email: leadcircle.bkbirlanightcollege@gmail.com
  • Platform: Campus Connect

We will make reasonable efforts to respond to privacy-related enquiries in a timely manner.

16. Acceptance Of This Policy

By accessing, registering for or using Campus Connect services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, you should discontinue use of the platform.

Privacy Commitment

Protecting Your Information

Campus Connect is committed to maintaining responsible privacy practices, protecting user information and continuously improving security measures.

Privacy, transparency and trust remain core principles of our platform and future development.
