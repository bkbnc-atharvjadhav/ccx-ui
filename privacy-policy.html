Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how Campus Connect collects, uses and protects your information.

Introduction

Campus Connect is committed to protecting the privacy of students, faculty members and institutions using our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used and safeguarded while using Campus Connect.

Information Collection

Information We Collect

Account Information

Name, email address, institutional information and profile details.

Academic Information

Attendance records, schedules, academic resources and related data.

Usage Information

Anonymous usage analytics used to improve platform performance and user experience.

How We Use Information

  • Provide platform functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Deliver notifications and updates
  • Support academic workflows
  • Maintain platform security
  • Improve system performance

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical, administrative and organizational safeguards to protect user information against unauthorized access, disclosure or misuse.

Third-Party Services

Campus Connect may use trusted third-party services for hosting, analytics, authentication, notifications and communication. These services are used solely to enhance platform functionality.

User Rights

  • Request access to your information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request account updates
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

Changes To This Policy

Campus Connect may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the platform after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Privacy Questions?

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your information, please contact us.
