Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we handle and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us such as your name, email address, and institutional credentials to provide our services. We also collect usage data to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to authenticate your identity, provide campus-specific notices, and track your academic progress. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. Please contact our support team for such requests.
