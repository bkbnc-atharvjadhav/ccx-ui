Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Campus Connect collects, uses, protects and manages information when users access our platform, website and services.

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Campus Connect is committed to protecting the privacy and security of students, faculty members, institutions and visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information that may be collected and how that information may be used to improve services and user experiences.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on platform usage, Campus Connect may collect the following categories of information:

  • User Profile Information
  • Name and Contact Details
  • Institution Information
  • Academic Information
  • Attendance Records
  • Device and Browser Information
  • Usage Analytics
  • Communication Preferences
  • Support Requests and Feedback

The information collected depends on the features and services being used.

3. How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • Provide platform functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Facilitate academic processes
  • Deliver notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Analyze platform performance
  • Enhance security and reliability
  • Develop future features and services

4. Data Security

Campus Connect implements reasonable administrative, technical and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction.

While no digital platform can guarantee absolute security, we continuously work towards maintaining secure and reliable systems.

5. User Rights

Users may have certain rights regarding their personal information, subject to applicable laws and institutional policies.

  • Request access to available information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request account updates
  • Request deletion where applicable
  • Manage communication preferences
  • Request clarification regarding data usage

6. Data Retention

Information may be retained for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with institutional requirements, fulfill legal obligations or support platform operations.

Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of the information and applicable institutional policies.

7. Third-Party Services

Campus Connect may integrate with third-party technologies and services to support functionality, analytics, notifications and infrastructure.

These providers may have their own privacy policies and practices. Users are encouraged to review applicable third-party policies where relevant.

8. Cookies & Analytics

The website and platform may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Improve performance
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze usage trends
  • Enhance user experience
  • Monitor platform reliability

Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

9. Changes To This Policy

Campus Connect may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect improvements, legal requirements or platform developments.

Updated versions will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or information practices, please contact us.
