Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Campus Connect collects, uses and protects information.

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

Campus Connect is committed to protecting the privacy and security of users. This policy describes the information we collect, how it is used and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

  • Name and profile information
  • Email address
  • Account credentials
  • Academic and attendance-related information
  • Usage and analytics data
  • Device and browser information

How Information Is Used

  • Provide platform functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Enhance security
  • Communicate important updates
  • Generate analytics and insights
  • Support academic engagement features

Data Protection

Campus Connect implements reasonable technical, administrative and organizational safeguards to help protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure or misuse.

Third-Party Services

Campus Connect may integrate with third-party services for authentication, hosting, notifications, analytics or communication.

These services operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

User Rights

  • Access personal information
  • Request correction of information
  • Request deletion where applicable
  • Receive information about data usage
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Cookies & Analytics

Campus Connect may use cookies, local storage and analytics tools to improve functionality and understand platform usage patterns.

Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations and support platform operations.

Changes To This Policy

Campus Connect may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, concerns or requests, please contact the Campus Connect team through the Contact page.
