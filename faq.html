Frequently Asked Questions

Answers To Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Campus Connect and our platform.

What is Campus Connect?

Campus Connect is a modern campus ERP, student engagement and administration platform designed to simplify institutional operations.

Who can use Campus Connect?

Students, faculty members, administrators and educational institutions.

Is Campus Connect available now?

Currently, access is available through pilot programs and early access requests.

Is there a mobile application?

Yes. Campus Connect is designed with a mobile-first experience in mind.

Can colleges request a demo?

Absolutely. Institutions can contact us for demonstrations and partnership discussions.

What features are included?

Attendance, timetable management, communication tools, analytics, student engagement and E-Cell features.

Does Campus Connect support events?

Yes. Event management and participation tracking are part of the ecosystem.

Is student data secure?

Security and privacy are important priorities, and platform access is managed through controlled permissions.

Can the platform be customized?

Future institutional customization options are part of the roadmap.
