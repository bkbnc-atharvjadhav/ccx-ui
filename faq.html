General Questions

Getting Started

What is Campus Connect?

Campus Connect is a smart campus ecosystem designed to simplify academics, communication, engagement and administration through a unified digital platform.

Who can use Campus Connect?

Students, faculty members, administrators, innovation cells and educational institutions.

Is Campus Connect free?

Availability and pricing may vary depending on institutional implementation and deployment models.

Which devices are supported?

Campus Connect supports Android, modern web browsers and future iOS deployments.

Platform Features

Features & Modules

What features does Campus Connect offer?

Attendance tracking, timetable management, announcements, events, analytics, innovation programs and communication tools.

Does Campus Connect support events?

Yes. Students can discover, register and participate in campus events and activities.

Are analytics available?

Yes. Institutions can access attendance analytics, engagement insights and reporting dashboards.

Can innovation cells use Campus Connect?

Absolutely. Innovation hubs, startup initiatives and hackathons are supported within the ecosystem.

Security & Privacy

Data Protection & Trust

Is student data secure?

Campus Connect is designed with security, privacy and responsible data management practices in mind to protect institutional information.

Who owns the data?

Educational institutions retain ownership and control over their organizational data and records.

Are user accounts protected?

User authentication and access controls help ensure that information remains accessible only to authorized users.

Does Campus Connect store sensitive information?

Data collection and storage practices depend on institutional deployment requirements and applicable policies.
