Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need To Know

Common questions about Campus Connect.

What is Campus Connect?
Campus Connect is a modern campus ERP and student engagement ecosystem.
Who can use Campus Connect?
Students, faculty, administrators and institutions.
Is Google Login supported?
Yes, Google authentication is supported.
Why is approval required?
Approval ensures a secure and verified campus ecosystem.
Can multiple colleges use Campus Connect?
Yes. Campus Connect supports a multi-tenant architecture.
Does Campus Connect support attendance tracking?
Yes. Attendance tracking is one of the core modules.
What are Learning Circles?
Collaborative communities for peer learning and engagement.
Is Campus Connect mobile friendly?
Yes. The platform is designed with a mobile-first approach.
