Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Campus Connect, all in one place.

What is Campus Connect? +
Campus Connect is a comprehensive "Super App" designed for modern campuses. It integrates academic management, attendance tracking, official notifications, and placement resources into a single, intuitive platform.
How do I get access to the app? +
If your institution is a partner, you can download the app from the Download page and log in using your official student credentials. If not, you can sign up for the beta waiting list.
Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow strict data privacy protocols to ensure that your academic and personal records are safe and accessible only to authorized personnel.
How does the attendance tracker work? +
The app syncs with the faculty's attendance logs. It provides a real-time percentage and alerts you if your attendance falls below the required threshold set by your department.
Can I use this for my own personal organization? +
While designed for institutional use, certain modules like the Timetable and Resource Library can be used independently for personal academic organization.
What should I do if I encounter a bug? +
We have a dedicated support system. You can report bugs via the Support page or send us an email at leadcircle.bkbirlanightcollege@gmail.com.
