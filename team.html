Our Story

How Campus Connect Was Born

Campus Connect started as an attempt to solve real-world problems experienced by students, faculty members and institutions every day.

Important announcements were often missed. Academic resources were scattered across different platforms. Students struggled to stay informed about events, opportunities and campus activities. Departments relied on multiple disconnected systems.

The vision was simple: Create one platform that brings everything together into a seamless campus experience. That vision eventually became Campus Connect.
