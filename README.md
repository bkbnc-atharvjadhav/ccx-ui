# Campus Connect Website

Official website for **Campus Connect** — a next-generation campus ERP, student engagement, and digital campus ecosystem.

## 🌐 Overview

Campus Connect is designed to unify academics, administration, communication, student engagement, entrepreneurship, attendance management, and institutional operations into a single platform.

This repository contains the official marketing and product website built to showcase the Campus Connect ecosystem, product features, roadmap, team, and platform vision.

---

## 🚀 About Campus Connect

Campus Connect serves as a centralized digital ecosystem for educational institutions.

The platform enables:

* Student Management
* Faculty Management
* Attendance Tracking
* Timetable Management
* Learning Circles
* Events & Activities
* Announcements & Notifications
* Messaging System
* Digital ID Cards
* Gamification & Rewards
* Entrepreneurship (E-Cell) Ecosystem
* Institutional Analytics
* Multi-College Administration
